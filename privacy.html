Effective Date: January 27, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information strictly to provide specialized sports analysis:

  • Account Data: Managed securely via our authentication partners.
  • User Content: Video clips you upload are processed temporarily to generate biomechanical feedback.

2. Third-Party Infrastructure

We utilize Tier-1 enterprise infrastructure to operate the service. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256):

  • Enterprise Neural Networks: We utilize industry-leading Large Multimodal Models (LMMs) to process video frames for biomechanical data extraction. No user video is used to train public models.
  • Secure Media Storage: Videos are stored in an encrypted media infrastructure layer.
  • Identity Management: Authentication is handled by SOC2-compliant identity providers.

3. Data Retention

You retain full ownership of your videos. You may delete specific clips or entire sessions from within the application dashboard at any time, which permanently removes them from our active processing queue.
